Needed ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • Pecorino
  • 180g Spaghetti
  • 300g Bacon
  • Parsley

How to cook Spagetti Carbonara in five steps:

  1. Put the Bacon in the pan on medium fire for 20 minutes
  2. Boil the water and cook the Spaghetti for 9 minutes
  3. Mix the egs with some water from the Spagetti
  4. Mix the egs with the pecorino cheese
  5. Mix the egs the bacon
  6. Cook for additional 3-5 minutes
